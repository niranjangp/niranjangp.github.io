Logo Designs

Logo

This Logo design is centered around my initials "N" "G" and "P". The design is mainly sharp and has geometric edges which is in-line with how my mind-map came along. I have mainly used the same color scheme as seen throughout the design system, keeping it simple and minimalistic, which is usually what I like.

Version: Light

This logo with a white background and dark characters will be used for situations that need better contrast which is, print services.

Version: Dark

This logo with a dark background and white characters will be used when users toggle their display to dark mode.

Version: Color

This logo is a colored version presented in contrasting colors and is the primary logo.

Version: Reverse

This logo, which is the reversal of the primary logo, acts as the secondary branding.

Version: Outline

This logo offers a simplified outline version, which can be used as tertiary branding or for lite versions.

Version: Color

This logo which is the primary version includes my first name as part of the logo.
